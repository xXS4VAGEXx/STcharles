Course Overview

University College London (UCL) offers a top-ranked Computer Science program that combines a solid foundation in theory with practical experience in cutting-edge technologies.

UCAS Code

G400

Tuition Fees

£9,250 per year (Home), £25,000 per year (International)

Required Grades

A*AA at A-Level

Location

UCL is situated in the heart of London, offering a vibrant and dynamic academic environment.

Core Computer Science Courses

Introduction to Programming

An introductory course focusing on software development, algorithms, and computational thinking.

Data Structures and Algorithms

This course covers essential topics like algorithms, data structures, and problem-solving techniques.

Machine Learning

A detailed study of machine learning techniques, including supervised and unsupervised learning, and their real-world applications.

Facilities

  • State-of-the-art computer labs and facilities
  • Access to high-performance computing resources for research and development
  • Advanced AI and machine learning labs
  • Dedicated spaces for collaboration and innovation

Application Process

To apply to UCL's Computer Science program, visit the UCAS website. A strong personal statement, academic references, and specific qualifications are required.
