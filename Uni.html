University of Oxford

Rank: #1 in the UK for Computer Science

Courses:

  • BSc Computer Science: Algorithms, Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Software Verification, Computer Security.
  • BSc Computer Science and Philosophy: Logic, Machine Learning, Philosophy of Mind, Computation Theory.

Student Life:

  • Transportation: Extensive cycling routes, bus services, and proximity to London by train.
  • Accommodation: College accommodation guaranteed for most undergraduates.
  • Finances: Oxford bursaries and scholarships available for eligible students.

Imperial College London

Rank: #2 in the UK for Computer Science

Courses:

  • BEng Computing: Software Engineering, Databases, Computer Architecture, AI, Robotics.
  • MEng Computing (Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning): Deep Learning, Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing.
  • MEng Computing (Software Engineering): Project Management, Software Development Practices, Advanced Programming Techniques.

Student Life:

  • Transportation: Located in Central London with excellent underground and bus networks.
  • Accommodation: Halls of residence available for first-year students; private housing options nearby.
  • Finances: Imperial College hardship funds and bursaries available.

University of Cambridge

Rank: #3 in the UK for Computer Science

Courses:

  • BA Computer Science: Machine Learning, Cybersecurity, Human-Computer Interaction, Computer Graphics.
  • Computer Science with Mathematics: Algorithms and Data Structures, Mathematical Methods, AI and Complexity Theory.

Student Life:

  • Transportation: Great cycling infrastructure, buses, and train access to London.
  • Accommodation: College-based accommodation usually guaranteed for the entire undergraduate course.
  • Finances: Cambridge bursary scheme supports eligible students with grants and scholarships.

University College London (UCL)

Rank: #5 in the UK for Computer Science

Courses:

  • BSc Computer Science: Machine Learning, Web Development, Networks and Security, Interaction Design.
  • MEng Computer Science: Research Project, Advanced AI, Distributed Systems, Entrepreneurship in Technology.

Student Life:

  • Transportation: Central London location; excellent underground, bus, and cycling facilities.
  • Accommodation: Guaranteed halls for first-year undergraduates; wide choice of private rentals.
  • Finances: UCL offers scholarships, bursaries, and hardship funds.

University of Edinburgh

Rank: #6 in the UK for Computer Science

Courses:

  • BSc Artificial Intelligence and Computer Science: Machine Learning, Computer Vision, Intelligent Robotics, Cognitive Science.
  • BSc Computer Science: Algorithms, Data Science, Programming Languages, Systems and Software Engineering.

Student Life:

  • Transportation: Excellent bus services across the city; student travel discounts available.
  • Accommodation: University accommodation for first-years; private rentals are common later on.
  • Finances: Scholarships and bursary options for domestic and international students.
