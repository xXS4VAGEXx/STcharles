Course Overview

The University of Cambridge offers a world-renowned Computer Science program that emphasizes both theoretical and practical skills in computing.

UCAS Code

G400

Tuition Fees

£9,250 per year (Home), £22,227 per year (International)

Required Grades

A*AA at A-Level

Location

Cambridge is located in the East of England, a vibrant academic city known for its university and intellectual culture.

Computer Science Courses

Fundamentals of Computer Science

A broad introduction to the discipline, focusing on programming, algorithm design, and computational theory.

Data Science & Big Data Analytics

Explore large-scale data analysis, statistical methods, and the techniques used to extract valuable insights from big data.

Systems and Network Security

Learn about network architectures, security protocols, encryption, and the techniques for protecting systems from cyber threats.

Facilities

  • Cutting-edge computer labs with industry-standard software
  • Access to high-performance computing for research purposes
  • Modern lecture theatres and seminar rooms
  • Specialized AI research centers and data science labs

Application Process

Apply through UCAS. A personal statement, references, and academic qualifications are required. An interview may be part of the selection process.
