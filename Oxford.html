Course Overview

The University of Oxford offers a highly regarded Computer Science course, which covers core concepts and cutting-edge technology in the field.

UCAS Code

G400

Tuition Fees

£9,250 per year (Home), £26,770 per year (International)

Required Grades

A*AA at A-Level

Location

Oxford is located in the Southeast of England, known for its prestigious university, historic buildings, and green spaces.

Computer Science Courses

Core Computer Science (First Year)

This course provides a solid foundation in computing, including algorithm design, programming, and theory.

Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning

Explore algorithms that enable computers to learn and adapt. Topics include machine learning, deep learning, and reinforcement learning.

Software Engineering

Focus on the methods and techniques used in large-scale software development, covering project management and software testing.

Facilities

  • State-of-the-art computer labs with latest technology
  • Dedicated AI and robotics laboratories
  • Access to high-performance computing clusters
  • Collaborative innovation hubs and study spaces

Application Process

Applications must be submitted via UCAS. Applicants need a strong personal statement, excellent academic qualifications, and must take the Oxford Computer Science Admissions Test (OXAT).
