Military Careers (Army, Navy, RAF, Marines)

Grade Requirements: Varies — entry roles no qualifications, technical roles may require GCSEs or A-levels (STEM preferred).

Deadlines: Rolling recruitment. Full selection process can take 6–12 months: application, medical, fitness tests, assessment centre.

Opportunities:

  • Fully funded apprenticeships in Engineering, IT, Logistics, Medical roles.
  • Free degree pathways after initial service (often STEM or Leadership degrees).
  • Early leadership roles from 18 years old — Officer route requires A-Levels or degree.
  • Travel opportunities (deployments, humanitarian missions, exercises abroad).
  • Starting Salaries: £18,687 (private) to £27,818 (officer cadet) + free housing, medical, meals.

Application Tip: Fitness is crucial — start practicing run times and strength tests early.

Other Apprenticeships (Advanced, Higher, Degree)

Grade Requirements:

  • Advanced: 5 GCSEs incl. Maths & English (Grade 4-9).
  • Higher: 2 A-Levels or equivalent.
  • Degree: 96-120 UCAS points (AAB - CCC).

Deadlines: Applications open year-round but major companies like BBC, EY, Amazon, BT, Siemens recruit Sept–Feb.

Opportunities:

  • Work in sectors: Engineering, Cybersecurity, Finance, Digital Marketing, Health, Construction.
  • Earn £15,000–£25,000 per year while studying (no debt).
  • Full-time job offers after completion are very common.
